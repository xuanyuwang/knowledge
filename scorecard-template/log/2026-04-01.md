## Details

### Why D+C over Hybrid B+D?

1. AutoQA forces creating a real option anyway ‚Üí having `naScore` alongside is redundant dual-store
2. i18n means label "N/A" isn't reliable ‚Üí need `isNA` flag (from Approach C)
3. Single source of truth: score lives in `scores[]` array, not a separate field

### Analytics Compatibility

Two-layer filtering in analytics handles scored N/A naturally:
- **Row filter** (`common_clickhouse.go:622-637`): `includeNaScored` controls `not_applicable <> true`
- **Aggregation filter** (`retrieve_qa_score_stats_clickhouse.go:151-152`): `percentage_value >= 0`
- Legacy N/A: `percentage_value = -1` ‚Üí excluded from SUM even when included in result set
- Scored N/A: `percentage_value >= 0` ‚Üí included in weighted SUM when `includeNaScored = true`

### BE Scoring Changes (2 small changes)

2. `ComputeCriterionPercentageScore()`: `||` ‚Üí `&&` (only skip when N/A AND no numeric values)
