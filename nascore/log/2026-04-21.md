## Details

### Validation fix for # of Occurrences with scored N/A

**Bug:** Saving a # of Occurrences criterion with a scored N/A option triggered "Behavior Score configuration is invalid."

**Root cause:** `validateNumOccurrencesScoreTypeConfigurationItem` in `validation.ts` (line 342-344) compared `auto_qa.options.length` with `settings.scores.length` and `settings.options.length`. When N/A is scored, it's added to `settings.options`/`settings.scores` but NOT to `auto_qa.options` (N/A is tracked separately via `auto_qa.not_applicable`). The length mismatch (e.g., 3 auto_qa options vs 4 settings options) caused the validation to fail.

**Fix:** Filter out N/A entries from `settings.options` and `settings.scores` before the length comparison, since N/A doesn't have a corresponding `auto_qa.options` entry.
