## Key Finding

AutoQA-created scorecards bypass the coaching `CreateScorecard` API. Live AutoQA and backfill both write through shared scoring persistence after `MapToScores(...)` and `ComputeScores(...)`. Therefore, a rule such as "scorecards must have at least one score" needs to be enforced in those paths or in the shared persistence helper.
