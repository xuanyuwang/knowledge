### Root Cause of 35 Remaining Missing Scorecards

Investigated why 35 scorecards on Mar 13 (24), Mar 23 (1), Mar 27 (10) were NOT recovered by the per-day backfill.

**Key finding: 34 of 35 have empty `conversation_id`** (empty string, not NULL).

| Submit Date | Total Missing | No Conv ID | Has Conv ID |
|-------------|--------------|------------|-------------|
| 2026-03-13  | 24           | 24         | 0           |
| 2026-03-23  | 1            | 0          | 1           |
| 2026-03-27  | 10           | 10         | 0           |

**Original hypothesis was WRONG**: It's not about conversation date mismatch. It's about scorecards with no conversation link at all.

**How the backfill works**: `reindexconversations` has TWO mechanisms:
1. **Conversation-based**: Finds conversations in date range, re-syncs all linked scorecards. This ran successfully for all days including Mar 13/27 (synced 36 and 35 conv-linked scorecards respectively).
2. **Orphan scorecard batch**: A separate batch step that syncs no-conv-id scorecards. This ran at `20:25:xx` UTC on Apr 9 and processed most days sequentially, but **skipped Mar 13 and Mar 27** for unknown reasons.

Evidence:
- All other days' no-conv-id scorecards WERE synced by the backfill (timestamps `20:25:09` to `20:25:37`, sequential by date)
- Mar 13 and Mar 27 no-conv-id scorecards are completely absent from the backfill output
- The 2 no-conv scorecards that DO exist in CH for Mar 13 were from the original real-time sync (Mar 31 and Apr 1), not the backfill

**The 1 Mar 23 scorecard with conv_id**: Created on Mar 13, submitted on Mar 23, conversation from Mar 13. The Mar 23 backfill couldn't find it because the conversation date is Mar 13, and the Mar 13 backfill processed it as a conversation-linked scorecard but didn't re-sync it (possibly because it wasn't submitted yet at the time of the conversation's creation, or the orphan batch skipped it).

**These are "standalone" scorecards**: Process IDs like "VI36:...", "708666532", "CS Number: 761209129" suggest external QA system integration (possibly Verint). They are QA scorecards created without linking to a Cresta conversation.

**Fix needed**: ID-based backfill targeting these 35 specific scorecard IDs, bypassing the conversation-date-range approach. The `reindexscorecards` workflow or a direct CH insert is required.

### Auto-Heal Design Doc

Created `auto-heal-design.md` — formal design doc following Cresta template. Key decisions:
- New Temporal workflow `backfill-scorecards-by-id` (not extending existing `reindexscorecards`)
- Cron task triggers workflow directly via `temporalClient.ExecuteWorkflow()` (no InternalJobService)
- Count-first optimization: only fetch IDs when counts mismatch
- Triage: categorize missing scorecards as conversation-linked vs standalone
- Batch at 500 IDs per workflow
- Metrics via `shared-go/framework/stats` → Groundcover/Datadog dashboards
- 3 phases, ~4 SWE-weeks total
