## Details

### Why the Change

`NewGroupedAppealWorkflowsFromScorecardIDs` uses dual recursive CTEs (upward + downward) to handle arbitrary starting points in the appeal chain. Our use case only needs forward traversal from originals → resolves, so a simple 3-JOIN query is much more efficient.

### Reference Pattern

`FindOriginalScorecardForAppealResolve()` in `action_submit_scorecard.go:293-326` — same 3-JOIN approach but in reverse direction (resolve → original). Our function mirrors this going forward (original → resolve).
